Rock Paper Scissors

How to play
Click the “Let's Play” button.
Choose between Rock, Paper or Scissor by clicking on the pictures.
Your opponent, The computer, randomly selects rock, paper, or scissors.
If both choices are the same - it's a tie.
If the player beats the computer - player score increases.
If the computer beats the player - computer score increases.
The scores and result message are updated in the DOM.
First to reach 5 points wins.
When someone wins, the choice buttons disappear and a Replay button appears.
